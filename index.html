BadgerBOTS Robotics

Co-President • 2013-2018

The FIRST Robotics Competition is an opportunity for students to work together to solve a common problem. We design (CAD - SolidWorks), build and program our robot which has advanced to the global competition the past two years. My sophomore year, I developed a web app that allowed our team to gather competitive data with our phones instead of paper. It used php and a centeralized MYSQL database. The system also compiles and generalizes the data so that we can quickly and effectively determine team strategy with the most recent data right before each match. My Junior year, I led the overall programming effort for the team. During my Senior year, I became the co-president of the team. In this role, I took on many new challenges like finances and buisness while still helping out with the robot.

Rocket Club

Lead Payload Monitoring System Engineer • 2016-2018

The rocket club is part of the Rockets for Schools program which builds a 7-foot rocket with a payload inside. Our team participates in launches every year with other teams and industry professionals. My role is to lead a subgroup focused on developing a comprehensive monitoring system. During my first year on the team, my team created our first monitoring system which measured acceleration, gyroscopic forces, and magnetic forces. In my second year, I created a much more advanced system that measured altitude and counted muons (a very useful subatomic particle). More info on these projects are located in my portfolio.
